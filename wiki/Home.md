# AI Compliance Framework — Wiki

Welcome to the official wiki for the [AI Compliance Framework](https://github.com/diShine-digital-agency/ai-compliance-framework), an open-source, practitioner-oriented resource for adopting AI responsibly in European corporate environments.

This wiki complements the repository's templates, guides, and tools with in-depth explanations of the regulatory landscape, practical compliance guidance, and reference material for anyone navigating AI governance in the EU.

---

## How to Use This Wiki

- **If you are new to AI compliance:** Start with the [EU AI Act Overview](EU-AI-Act-Overview) and then read [About This Framework](About-This-Framework) to understand what the repository provides.
- **If you are implementing the framework:** Go to the [Repository Guide](Repository-Guide) for a map of every template and guide, then follow the [Step-by-Step User Guide](https://github.com/diShine-digital-agency/ai-compliance-framework/blob/main/guides/how-to-use-this-framework.md) in the repository.
- **If you need regulatory detail:** Use the topic pages below for deep dives into specific regulations, obligations, and processes.
- **If you need a quick definition:** See the [Glossary](Glossary).
