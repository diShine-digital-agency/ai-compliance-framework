# EU AI Act Enforcement Timeline

The EU AI Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689) entered into force on 1 August 2024. Its obligations are phased in over three years. This page lists every key compliance date.

## Timeline at a Glance

```
Aug 2024 ───── Entry into force
     │
Feb 2025 ───── Prohibited practices banned; AI literacy obligation in effect
     │
Aug 2025 ───── GPAI obligations apply; governance bodies operational;
     │         Member States designate national authorities
     │
Feb 2026 ───── Commission guidance on high-risk classification due
     │
Aug 2026 ───── High-risk AI requirements enforced; full penalty regime active;
     │         EU AI Database registration required
     │
Aug 2027 ───── Full application to legacy systems in regulated products
```

## Detailed Date Reference

### 2024

| Date | Obligation | Who Is Affected |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| **1 August 2024** | EU AI Act enters into force (published in OJ 12 July 2024) | All |

### 2025

| Date | Obligation | Who Is Affected |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| **2 February 2025** | Prohibitions on unacceptable-risk AI practices take effect (Article 5). Penalties for prohibited practices are enforceable. | All providers and deployers |
| **2 February 2025** | AI literacy obligation takes effect (Article 4). Organisations must ensure staff dealing with AI systems have sufficient AI literacy. | All providers and deployers |
| **2 August 2025** | GPAI model obligations take effect (Articles 51–55). Providers of GPAI models must comply with transparency, copyright, and (where applicable) systemic risk requirements. | GPAI model providers |
| **2 August 2025** | Member States must designate national market surveillance authorities and notifying authorities. | Member States |
| **2 August 2025** | The EU AI Board becomes operational. | EU institutions |
| **2 August 2025** | The European AI Office's enforcement powers for GPAI are active. | GPAI model providers |
| **2 August 2025** | Penalty provisions for GPAI non-compliance activate. | GPAI model providers |
| **2 August 2025** | GPAI Code of Practice published (final version July 2025); adherence provides a rebuttable presumption of compliance. | GPAI model providers |

### 2026

| Date | Obligation | Who Is Affected |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| **2 February 2026** | European Commission must publish guidelines on the practical implementation of the high-risk classification (Article 6(5)). | European Commission |
| **2 February 2026** | Commission must publish guidelines on prohibited practices. | European Commission |
| **2 August 2026** | **Main enforcement date.** High-risk AI system requirements (Articles 9–15) take full effect. | Providers and deployers of high-risk AI |
| **2 August 2026** | Transparency obligations for limited-risk systems (Article 50) take full effect. | Providers and deployers |
| **2 August 2026** | EU AI Database registration required for high-risk systems (Article 49). | Providers and deployers of high-risk AI |
| **2 August 2026** | Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment required for deployers in specified categories (Article 27). | Deployers of high-risk AI |
| **2 August 2026** | Full penalty regime active: up to €35M / 7% (prohibited), €15M / 3% (high-risk), €7.5M / 1% (incorrect information). | All |
| **2 August 2026** | Post-market monitoring and serious incident reporting obligations take effect (Articles 72–73). | Providers and deployers of high-risk AI |
| **2 August 2026** | Enforcement actions for GPAI models by the AI Office (model recalls, information requests) become available. | GPAI model providers |
| **December 2026** | Transposition deadline for the revised Product Liability Directive 2024/2853 into national law. | Member States, all market participants |

### 2027

| Date | Obligation | Who Is Affected |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| **2 August 2027** | Full application to **legacy high-risk AI systems** embedded in regulated products covered by Annex I harmonisation legislation (e.g., medical devices, machinery, civil aviation). | Providers of legacy systems |
| **2 August 2027** | GPAI models placed on the market before 2 August 2025 must be brought into compliance. | GPAI model providers (legacy models) |

## The Digital Omnibus

In late 2025, the European Commission proposed the **Digital Omnibus on AI Regulation**, which includes modest timeline extensions for certain high-risk AI system obligations. The primary purpose was to grant additional compliance time where Member States had not yet finalised national implementing legislation or designated authorities. This does not change the core August 2026 enforcement date for most obligations but may affect specific transitional arrangements.

## Practical Implications

- **The most urgent deadline for most organisations is 2 August 2026**, when high-risk system requirements and the full penalty regime take effect.
- **AI literacy and prohibited practice compliance are already mandatory** as of February 2025.
- **GPAI providers have been subject to their obligations since August 2025.**
- Organisations should use the period before August 2026 to complete their compliance programme. The repository's [Step-by-Step User Guide](https://github.com/diShine-digital-agency/ai-compliance-framework/blob/main/guides/how-to-use-this-framework.md) maps this process across a 90-day implementation plan.
