# EU AI Act Enforcement Timeline

The EU AI Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689) entered into force on 1 August 2024. Its obligations are phased in through 2030. This page lists every key compliance date and reflects the **Digital Omnibus on AI Regulation** proposal (adopted by the European Parliament on 26 March 2026 and by the Council on 13 March 2026; trilogue political agreement expected 28 April 2026).

> **⚖️ Status caveat (April 2026):** The 2 November 2026, 2 December 2027, and 2 August 2028 dates below reflect the Digital Omnibus proposed timeline. **Until the Omnibus is formally adopted and published in the Official Journal (targeted July 2026),** 2 August 2026 remains the legally binding date for Annex III high-risk obligations and Article 50 transparency obligations. Plan for the earlier of the two timelines.

## Timeline at a Glance

```
Aug 2024 ───── Entry into force
     │
Feb 2025 ───── Prohibited practices banned; AI literacy obligation in effect ✅
     │
Aug 2025 ───── GPAI obligations apply; governance bodies operational;
     │         Member States designate national authorities ✅
     │
Feb 2026 ───── Commission guidance on high-risk classification due ✅
     │
Aug 2026 ───── National AI regulatory sandboxes must be operational (Art. 57)
     │
Nov 2026 ───── Article 50 transparency & watermarking obligations apply *(Omnibus)*
     │
Dec 2026 ───── Revised Product Liability Directive transposition deadline (9 Dec)
     │
Aug 2027 ───── Legacy GPAI models must comply
     │
Dec 2027 ───── High-risk Annex III obligations fully apply *(Omnibus)*
     │
Aug 2028 ───── High-risk AI in regulated products (Annex I / Art. 6.1) *(Omnibus)*
     │
Aug 2030 ───── Legacy public-sector AI systems must comply
```

## Detailed Date Reference

### 2024

| Date | Obligation | Who Is Affected |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| **1 August 2024** | EU AI Act enters into force (published in OJ 12 July 2024) | All |

### 2025 ✅ (in force)

| Date | Obligation | Who Is Affected |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| **2 February 2025** | Prohibitions on unacceptable-risk AI practices take effect (Article 5). Penalties for prohibited practices are enforceable. | All providers and deployers |
| **2 February 2025** | AI literacy obligation takes effect (Article 4). Organisations must ensure staff dealing with AI systems have sufficient AI literacy. | All providers and deployers |
| **2 August 2025** | GPAI model obligations take effect (Articles 51–55). Providers of GPAI models must comply with transparency, copyright, and (where applicable) systemic risk requirements. | GPAI model providers |
| **2 August 2025** | Member States must designate national market surveillance authorities and notifying authorities. | Member States |
| **2 August 2025** | The EU AI Board becomes operational. | EU institutions |
| **2 August 2025** | The European AI Office's enforcement powers for GPAI are active. | GPAI model providers |
| **2 August 2025** | Penalty provisions for GPAI non-compliance activate. | GPAI model providers |
| **10 July 2025** | GPAI Code of Practice final version published; adherence provides a rebuttable presumption of compliance. | GPAI model providers |

### 2026

| Date | Obligation | Who Is Affected |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| **2 February 2026** ✅ | European Commission guidelines on the practical implementation of the high-risk classification (Article 6(5)) and on prohibited practices. | European Commission |
| **2 August 2026** | **National AI regulatory sandboxes must be operational** (Article 57). | Member States |
| **2 November 2026** *(Omnibus)* | Transparency obligations for limited-risk systems (Article 50) take full effect, including AI-generated content watermarking requirements. | Providers and deployers |
| **9 December 2026** | Transposition deadline for the revised Product Liability Directive (Directive 2024/2853) into national law. | Member States, all market participants |

### 2027

| Date | Obligation | Who Is Affected |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| **2 August 2027** | GPAI models placed on the market before 2 August 2025 must be brought into compliance. | GPAI model providers (legacy models) |
| **2 December 2027** *(Omnibus)* | **Main enforcement date for high-risk Annex III systems.** Articles 9–15 requirements take full effect. EU AI Database registration (Art. 49), FRIA (Art. 27), post-market monitoring and serious-incident reporting (Arts. 72–73) apply. Full penalty regime active: up to €35M / 7% (prohibited), €15M / 3% (high-risk), €7.5M / 1% (incorrect information). | Providers and deployers of high-risk AI |

### 2028

| Date | Obligation | Who Is Affected |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| **2 August 2028** *(Omnibus)* | High-risk AI systems embedded in regulated products (Annex I / Article 6(1)) — medical devices, machinery, civil aviation, vehicles, etc. — must comply. | Providers of regulated-product AI |

### 2030

| Date | Obligation | Who Is Affected |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| **2 August 2030** | Legacy public-sector AI systems in use before 2 August 2026 must comply. | Public-sector deployers |
| **31 December 2030** | Large-scale IT system AI components (Annex IX — SIS, VIS, EES, ETIAS, Eurodac) must comply. | EU institutions / MS agencies |

## The Digital Omnibus on AI Regulation

In November 2025 the European Commission published the **Digital Omnibus on AI Regulation** proposal, designed to rebalance the enforcement calendar in light of delayed harmonised standards and incomplete national implementation.

**Key proposed changes** (as reflected in this timeline):

- **Annex III high-risk obligations** moved from 2 August 2026 to **2 December 2027** (standalone long-stop date; shorter if harmonised standards are published earlier).
- **Regulated-product high-risk** (Annex I / Art. 6(1)) moved to **2 August 2028**.
- **Article 50 transparency** obligations moved to **2 November 2026**.
- **Article 57 sandbox deadline** (2 August 2026) is retained.

**Legislative status (April 2026):**

- 13 March 2026 — Council general approach adopted.
- 18–19 March 2026 — EP IMCO / LIBE committees jointly adopted their report (101–9–8).
- 26 March 2026 — EP plenary mandate confirmed; trilogue opened.
- 28 April 2026 — political trilogue expected to close.
- May–June 2026 — formal EP + Council endorsement expected.
- July 2026 — publication in the Official Journal targeted.

**Until publication, the 2 August 2026 deadlines in the original Regulation 2024/1689 remain legally binding.** Organisations should continue to plan for the earlier of the two dates.

## Practical Implications

- **Already in force:** AI literacy and prohibited-practice compliance (Feb 2025); GPAI provider obligations (Aug 2025).
- **Most urgent upcoming deadlines:** national sandbox operability (Aug 2026), Article 50 transparency (Nov 2026 — Omnibus), PLD transposition (9 Dec 2026).
- **Main compliance target:** 2 December 2027 (Omnibus) or 2 August 2026 (pre-Omnibus) — whichever survives trilogue and publication.
