## EU Charter of Fundamental Rights

The EU Charter underpins the FRIA and many AI Act provisions. Key articles for AI compliance:

| Article | Right |
|---|---|
| 1 | Human dignity |
| 7 | Respect for private and family life |
| 8 | Protection of personal data |
| 11 | Freedom of expression and information |
| 21 | Non-discrimination |
| 24 | Rights of the child |
| 26 | Integration of persons with disabilities |
| 27–31 | Workers' rights |
| 41 | Right to good administration |
| 47 | Right to an effective remedy and to a fair trial |

[Full text of the EU Charter](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/treaty/char_2012/oj)
